Back 2 School

A mobile app that helps parents find a school for their children to enroll in. The app enables the parents to search for a school according to certain priorities that resonate with them. They can also book a date to tour the school and to check out the facilities of a school. They are also able to leave comments on certain school pages and communicate with other parents and hear their opinions.

Chanuka

Chanuka provides age based, interactive money lessons for children 10 -12 years old (5th and 6th grade). It is a 5 week interactive live course taught online via Zoom where children learn about personal finance.
